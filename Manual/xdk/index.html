Manual of the XDG Development Kit (XDK)

Next: Table of contents, Up: (dir)

The XDG Development Kit (XDK) is a grammar development environment for the grammar formalism Extensible Dependency Grammar (XDG). It was written by Ralph Debusmann (rade@ps.uni-sb.de), Denys Duchier (duchier@ps.uni-sb.de), Jorge Marques Pelizzoni (jpeliz@icmc.usp.br) and Jochen Setz (info@jochensetz.de). The long list of other contributors to the XDK can be found here (Contributors).
